Alaskan Shore Bird Microbiota Project

Study


  • As part of the Alaskan shore bird microbiota project at Kansas State University, avian fecal samples were collected from nine sites along the Alaskan shore and Canada.
  • The project was conducted as part of a research study focusing on the evolving diversity of bird gut microbes.


Analysis


  • The samples where processed with Illumina DNA sequencing.
  • The sequencing results (.fastq files) were analyzed with Mothur, an Open-Source, Platform-Independent, Community-Supported Software for Describing and Comparing Microbial Communities.
